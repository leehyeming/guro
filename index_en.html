STEP 01

Pureun Arboretum

⏱ ~ 2 Hours Recommended

Seoul's first municipal arboretum, spanning 60,000 pyeong. A massive central lawn, serene wetland boardwalks, and a fragrant rose garden offer an oasis of deep relaxation.

💡 Zero-waste park: no trash cans inside — Please bring a personal bag to carry your trash.
🔗 Official Blog
Visit Blog →
STEP 02

Hangdong Pureun Library

⏱ Library in the Forest | Free Explore

A unique library nestled inside the arboretum. Read a book surrounded by floor-to-ceiling windows showing nature's seasonal beauty, offering a quiet, healing retreat.

💡 Connected to the arboretum trail; we highly recommend the windows side seats for the best view.

STEP 03

KB Forest Education Center

⏱ ~ 15 Minutes

An exotic glass greenhouse featuring tropical plants and diverse herbs. The humid, fresh air and scent of plants soothe the mind.

💡 Offers clean restrooms and a cozy indoor rest area, making it a perfect midway break spot.

STEP 04

Hangdong Railroad

⏱ ~ 25 Minutes

A single-track cargo railroad built in 1959. Walk along the rusted tracks surrounded by wild grass and capture nostalgic photos with friends.

💡 Very little shade; hats or umbrellas are recommended. Bicycles and kickboards are prohibited for safety.

STEP 05 · FINAL

Oryu Beodeul Town

⏱ ~ 2+ Hours (Dining & Coffee)

A local brand hub where nostalgic alleys meet trendy restaurants, neighborhood workshops, and cozy brunch cafes, newly shaped by young local creators.

💡 Stores are spread across multiple alleys; taking your time to explore on foot is highly recommended.

Travel Guide

Crucial tips and information for a safe and relaxing walking tour in Guro

🚌

Transportation

  • Subway: Line 1 or 7, Onsu Station (Exit 3), then transfer to local bus.
  • Bus Stop: Pureun Arboretum Back Gate / Hangdong Harborline Apt.
  • We recommend comfortable sneakers as most paths consist of trails and walkways.
  • Transit: Taking a local bus (approx. 10m) from the railway to Beodeul Town is recommended.
⚠️

Precautions

  • Eco Policy: Arboretum has no trash bins to preserve nature; carry a trash bag with you.
  • Safety: Strollers, bicycles, and electric kickboards are strictly banned on the narrow railroad track.
  • Sun Protection: Railroad trails lack shade; bring sunglasses, sunscreen, and hats/parasols.
  • Shops: Local Beodeul Town boutiques have individual holidays; check before visiting.
💰

Estimated Cost

  • Entrance Fees: Pureun Arboretum, Library, and Greenhouse are completely Free.
  • Railway Walk: Public pathway and photography access are Free.
  • Dining & Cafe: Expect approx. 15,000 to 20,000 KRW (~$15 USD) per person for lunch/coffee.
Total: ~ 20,000 KRW / person

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We have compiled answers to the most common questions about the Guro healing walk.

The recommended route is: 1. Pureun Arboretum (approx. 2 hours) ➔ 2. Hangdong Pureun Library ➔ 3. KB Forest Education Center (approx. 15 mins) ➔ 4. Hangdong Railroad (approx. 25 mins) ➔ 5. Oryu Beodeul Town (approx. 2+ hours).

The total distance is about 4.2 km, and taking all rest, dining, and reading into account, it takes a total of approx. 5 hours.

  • Pureun Arboretum: There are absolutely no trash cans in the park. Please carry a bag to take your trash back with you.
  • Hangdong Railroad: Strollers, kickboards, and bicycles are forbidden on the ballast tracks. Due to the lack of shade, carrying parasols, sunglasses, and hats is strongly advised.

Representative local spots in the Oryu Beodeul Town alleys include:

  • Chewy potato dumplings in buckwheat broth: Onggijonggi Potato Ongsimi
  • Fresh grilled beef burgers with organic buns: Staple Burger
  • Crispy glazed chicken bites: 87 Sweet & Spicy Chicken
  • Warm, spicy sausage and ham stew: Byunggyu's Budae-jjigae
  • Cozy botanical pasta and brunch: Sasaenghwal Cafe and organic bakery Bread Hong
